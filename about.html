Beth and Jen meet in 2001 in Atlanta, Georgia. People always ask how we met,
and everyones loves a good story, but the short version is; we survived a cult together.
The best part through the trauma we endured, was walking away from it with a best friend
and sister for life. We were wives, mothers, providers, caretakers and spent our free time
hosting parties and events for friends and family. Between the both of us, we share 40
combined years of in the food and hospitality industry representing various positions,
including culinary and management. In 2018, Jen joined the film industry and talked to me
about it passionately, until I finally caved and joined her the following year. In 2019,
Jen proposed that we start a craft services company for the film industry and go into
business together doing what we know how to do best; take care of and love the people
around us. So, we put our heads and hearts together, purchased a food trailer and are
living our dream!
